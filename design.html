UX Design

Be simple. Be direct. Use pictures.

Strong visual communication (in combination with good writing) is essential when presenting an idea. As such, I have experience designing various diagrams, process workflows, and websites using products such as Visio, Adobe Photoshop, and good old fashion pen and paper. Below are samples taken from projects I have worked on.

Flowcharts and Diagrams

In documentation, diagrams can help summarize and explain the process and relationship of multiple functions in a simple and succinct manner. The examples below show diagrams that I have created to accompany written documentation. They were created primarily in Visio based on information gathering sessions between myself and system architects, developers, or product owners. 

I tend to prefer minimal colouring and favour contrast and proximity to convey visual information. This helps promote accessibility for users with colour-blindness or limited visual impairment and reduce reliance on colour printing (for printed guides and manuals).
