What I do

The work I do falls under various names -- technical writer, usability expert, information developer, to name a few -- but the end-goal is always the same; to facilitate the communication of technical and scientific information for an advanced-level audience group while managing stakeholder expectation and timelines.

Communication is an essential part of any company and good communication between products and users promotes quality results. Technical Communication is a field that combines strong communication skills with a comprehensive technical and scientific background to provide technology users clear and effective communication using various mediums. Products could range from a simple building poster to a complex technical specification sheet; However, the goal remains the same--to help people use these products easily and effectively, bridging the gap between technology and its users.
